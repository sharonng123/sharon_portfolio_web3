KuCoin Visual Design — Flagship Projects

Role: Visual Design Senior Officer Company: KuCoin Exchange

At KuCoin I led high-impact visual design for flagship products and campaigns. My work ensured consistent application of KuCoin’s visual brand identity across digital platforms, marketing materials, and offline channels, delivering a unified modern presence in Web3.

Key Achievements

  • Brand consistency: Led consistent application of KuCoin’s visual identity across app store assets, social platforms and offline activations
  • Cross-functional collaboration: Worked with marketing, product, branding, and engineering to align creative with business goals and launch timelines
  • Unified Web3 presence: Delivered a cohesive, modern look and feel across digital and physical touchpoints
  • Flagship deliverables: Motion graphics, social & meme content, Hong Kong Consensus 2026 booth, side events, KuCoin × Tomorrowland partnership materials, and App Store creatives
