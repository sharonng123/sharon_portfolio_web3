MTR Social Media & Campaign Graphics

Role: Digital Content Designer Duration: Aug 2022 - Oct 2023

I was responsible for creating 95% of the engaging graphics and content used on the social media accounts of Telford Plaza and Maritime Square. This extensive body of work focused on enhancing the shopping centers' digital presence, promoting retail events and offers, and building stronger connections with the local community through compelling visual storytelling.

Key Achievements

  • Content Ownership: Created 95% of all social media graphics and content for two major shopping centers
  • Brand Consistency: Maintained distinct visual identities for Telford Plaza and Maritime Square across all campaigns
  • Multi-Platform Optimization: Adapted designs for Facebook, Instagram, and other social media channels
  • Community Engagement: Applied behavioral insights to create content that resonates with shoppers and local residents
