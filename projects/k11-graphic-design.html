K11 Digital Marketing Graphics

Role: Digital Marketing Officer Duration: 2022

I collaborated with the design team to create graphics for pop-up banners and live stories within the K11 MUSEA app. These graphics were strategically designed to promote upcoming events and features, enhancing the digital experience for luxury retail customers and supporting the innovative "Spot the Avatar" NFT gamification campaign.

Key Achievements

  • Team Collaboration: Successfully collaborated with K11's design team to deliver high-quality app graphics
  • Brand Integration: Seamlessly integrated promotional graphics with K11's luxury aesthetic and app interface
  • Event Promotion: Created visually compelling pop-up banners and live stories that effectively promoted upcoming events
  • App Experience: Enhanced the K11 MUSEA app's visual appeal and user engagement through strategic graphic placement

Graphics Portfolio

A collection of digital marketing graphics created for K11 MUSEA app, featuring wide promotional banners, pop-up cards, and campaign materials designed for luxury retail events and brand activations. Displayed in their authentic app banner format.
