Project Overview

Role: UI/UX Designer | Duration: 3 months | Team: Cross-functional (Design, Development, Product)

As a UI/UX designer at Watsons, I led the enhancement of our AI chatbot to handle order-related inquiries more effectively. The project aimed to streamline customer experience across web and mobile platforms while reducing operational costs and improving service availability.

Competitive Research: AI Chatbot Benchmarking

I conducted in-depth research on AI chatbot implementations from Lazada, Cider, Sephora, and Amazon to understand current market standards and identify opportunities for Watsons' chatbot enhancement.

üõçÔ∏è Lazada AI Assistant

Key Features: Strong order tracking integration, multi-language support (EN/TH/MY), quick response times. Shows comprehensive order history and status tracking with clear visual hierarchy.

üëó Cider Customer Support Bot

Key Features: AI guide with step-by-step screens, survey button next to replies, live agent appears for specific questions. Mainly AI responses with visual guidelines for user assistance.

üíÑ Sephora Virtual Assistant

Key Features: Beauty product recommendations, personalized skincare advice, seamless booking integration. Focus on advisory-style conversations with rich product visuals and detailed guidance.

üåø iHerb AI Assistant

Key Features: Health and wellness focused AI support with product recommendations, supplement guidance, and order management. Demonstrates how health-focused brands can integrate AI assistance with specialized product knowledge.

üöÄ Key Insights for Watsons AI Chatbot Strategy

Based on the competitive analysis, here are the strategic directions for enhancing Watsons' AI chatbot capabilities:

  • Visual AI Guidance (Inspired by Cider): Implement step-by-step visual screens that guide users through complex processes like returns, cancellations, and order tracking. AI responses should include contextual screenshots and visual cues to reduce cognitive load.
  • Integrated Feedback System: Following Cider's approach, place survey buttons directly next to AI replies for immediate feedback collection. This enables real-time conversation quality assessment and rapid service improvement.
  • Smart Escalation Triggers: Like Cider's model, live agents should appear automatically when users have specific, complex questions that require human expertise. The AI should recognize conversation patterns that indicate escalation needs.
  • Quick Access Navigation (Inspired by Sephora): Implement quick link functionality for efficient delivery tracking and common order management tasks, reducing user friction and providing instant access to key information.
  • Advisory Conversation Style (Inspired by Lazada): Adopt conversational AI that offers product recommendations and guidance alongside order management, creating a more engaging and helpful customer experience.
  • Progressive Visual Disclosure: Instead of overwhelming text menus, use visual cards and progressive disclosure to guide users through options, making the experience more intuitive and less intimidating.
