The Challenge: When Customers Can't Get Answers

Enhancing Watsons' chatbot to handle complex order-related inquiries

At Watsons, our existing chatbot could handle basic FAQs, but customers were frustrated when trying to get help with order-related issues. This created a bottleneck in customer service and led to longer wait times for human agents.

The Problem Statement:

  • Customers couldn't get real-time order status updates through chat
  • Delivery inquiries required human agent intervention
  • Order cancellation and return processes were unclear and manual
  • Long waiting times during peak hours affected customer satisfaction

Project Goals:

  • Streamline Web and App customer chat experience across all touchpoints
  • Shorten waiting time with 24/7 AI-powered support
  • Increase chatbot resolution rate for order-related inquiries
  • Reduce human agent workload by handling routine order questions

Key Learnings & Design Insights

Critical discoveries from designing AI-powered customer support

  • Context is King: Customers expect the chatbot to understand their order history and previous interactions, not start from scratch each time
  • Visual Beats Text: Showing order information visually (cards, timelines, images) is far more effective than text-only responses
  • Proactive Communication: The best chatbot experiences anticipate user needs and offer relevant actions before being asked
  • Graceful Failure: When AI can't help, the handoff to human agents must be seamless with full context transfer
  • Cultural Sensitivity: Different markets have different communication styles and expectations for customer service
  • Progressive Disclosure: Showing all information at once overwhelms users; revealing details step-by-step works better
  • Trust Through Transparency: Users need to understand what the chatbot can and cannot do to set proper expectations

Next Steps & Future Enhancements

  • Voice Integration: Adding voice input/output for hands-free interactions
  • Predictive Support: Proactively reaching out about potential delivery issues
  • Personalization Engine: Learning individual customer preferences and communication styles
  • Integration Expansion: Connecting with more backend systems for comprehensive support
